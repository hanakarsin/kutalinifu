Abacha's Daughter Tempted To Join APC

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Saturday, March 30, 2024

The All Progressives Congress (APC) appears to be amassing more supporters on a daily basis.

It appears to be the reigning party with their glaring victory at the just concluded elections.

Just as they are gaining the admiration of Nigerians, they are also gaining that of politicians from rival parties, who have been dumping their parties for the APC.

Seems like everybody wants to join the party, which preaches ‘Change.’

Gumsu Sani Abacha, one of the daughters of late military Head of State, Sani Abacha, is the latest individual to signify her interest in joining the party.

READ ALSO: Gumsu Sani Abacha Gets Lovely Kiss From Her Younger Sister

Gumsu wrote on her Facebook page, "I am so tempted to join the APC."

The APC  is set to take over from the ruling Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) on May 29 when the President-elect Muhammadu Buhari becomes the president.

Buhari and Abacha were part of the coup of July 1966 that overthrew the government of General Aguiyi-Ironsi.

Gumsu is married to Bayero Fadil, a billionaire businessman from Cameroon. The couple got married in 1999 and are blessed with five children.
