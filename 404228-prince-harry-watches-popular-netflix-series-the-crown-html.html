Prince Harry says he watches popular Netflix series The Crown

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Monday, February 5, 2024

- Former Duke of Sussex has revealed that he prefers watching a show that emulates the royal life rather than reading news about his family

- The 36-year-old appeared on a TV interview where he said he prefers to watch the popular Netflix show, The Crown

The former Duke and Duchess of Sussex have been trending online following news that the two will be having a sit down interview with media mogul Oprah Winfrey.

Ever since the two resigned from their royal duties, Prince Harry has been opening up to American media and this time round, he has disclosed what he likes to watch.

While appearing on The Late Late Show with host James Corden, the hunk father of one revealed that he prefers to watch the popular Netflix show The crown.

The 36-year-old prince mentioned to the host that he likes to watch the show as opposed to read news headlines about him and his American actress wife Meghan Markle.

His family has, however, had unkind words about the popular show because the storyline of the show centres on the royalty life and style.

Funs of the show have also mentioned that the latest season's storyline focuses on the prince's parent's estranged marriage.

Reports from publications in the UK had mentioned that his brother, the Duke of Cambridge is not happy with the latest season of The Crown as it paints his parents' marriage in bad light.

This is what The Daily Mail said about Prince Williams concern about the show's storyline on Prince Charles and Princess Diana.

“The Duke of Cambridge is none too pleased with it. He feels that both his parents are being exploited and being presented in a false, simplistic way to make money.”

But Harry has shown that he is least bothered with what the show depicts.

“They do not pretend to be news, it is fictional, but it is loosely based on the truth, it gives you a rough idea about what the pressures of putting duty and service above family and everything else is, what can come from that I am way more comfortable with The Crown than I am seeing the stories written about my family or my wife or myself. That is obviously fiction, take it how you will, but this is being reported on as fact because it is supposedly news. I have a real issue with that,” he said.

TUKO.co.ke had earlier reported that Harry and his wife had permanently resigned from royal duties.

In a recent statement, the Queen noted that the pair had decided not to resume their royal roles after taking a hiatus last year and this meant that Harry will now be stripped of all his titles, including his military ones.
