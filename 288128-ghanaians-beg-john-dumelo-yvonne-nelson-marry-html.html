Ghanaians Beg Yvonne Nelson And John Dumelo To Get Married

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, January 1, 0001

 Ghanaians have recently pleaded with award winning actress and actor, Yvonne Nelson and John Dumelo to get married, considering their public shows of affection that has heralded their friendship relationship.

The beautiful actress, Yvonne Nelson, has been urged to marry her long time friend and colleague, John Dumelo, instead of the frequent frolicking and uncalled rumors that has made their friendship to be more popular.

Pulse NG reports that a recent survey conducted by an authoritative Ghanaian entertainment website, reveals that the screen stars should actually get hooked up instead of continuing to fool themselves that they are 'friends while they keep doing things that ordinary friends should not do.'

A good percentage of the respondents of the survey, actually affirmed that Yvonne Nelson and John Dumelo are dating, but they keep denying and saying that there is nothing going on between them.

Below are what some of the respondents said:

Yvonne Nelson and John Dumelo should stop deceiving themselves and get married. We all know they are dating but they keep denying the fact but what they do behind the scene is quite obvious. So what are they hiding? - Kwame Derick

Those who know the two closely can attest to the fact that they are dating and sleeping together, so what is the point of them hiding it from the public? They are both adults and they should do the right thing instead of deceiving themselves.  - Esther Bossman

I am a movie person and I have been on set where I have seen Yvonne and John do things only lovers do, so I get surprised when they keep telling people they are mere friends. They are more than that ans should do the right thing and get married because they are of age and no one can tell them what to do.   - Kaaki O.

Well, all said and done, everything still lies in the court of these two adults. It would be recalled that just recently, the beautiful actress was spotted on the roads of Accra, sharing flyers in the traffic to the amazement of many road users.
