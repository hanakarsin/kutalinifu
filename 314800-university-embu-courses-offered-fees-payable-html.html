University of Embu - courses offered and fees payable

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Saturday, July 6, 2024

The University of Embu (UoEm) is a constituent college of the University of Nairobi established in 2011. It is an institution of higher learning and the epicentre of excellence in research and training. Over the years, the university has focused on innovation as the engine of its institutional development. As a public university, UoEm continues to facilitate and encourage novelty in technologies and ideas in its endeavours.

The University of Embu commits to providing high-quality education, training, research, and extension services. Students who enrol at the institution can get many courses that prepare them for the job market. Also, the courses available impact knowledge and skills students need to face modern challenges.

Embu University has many academic programmes. You can enrol for a PhD, Master’s Degree, Bachelor’s Degree, Diploma, or Certificate course. All courses offered at the University of Embu are available either on full-time or part-time modes.

Scholarships offered at the University of Embu

The University of Embu offers postgraduate scholarships every year for qualifying students. Its scholarship is an internally funded programme offered to those that want to further their education in the fields of Agriculture, Pure and Applied Sciences, Business and Economics, and Education and Social Sciences.

A student that qualifies for a scholarship will only complete their studies in Kenya. Also, you must be a Kenyan and a UoEm graduate to be eligible for the award. As an applicant, you must have attained a minimum of a Second Class Honours in any relevant degree to qualify.

The package covers the full tuition fees and stipends throughout the study period. You can obtain the scholarship application form from the university’s website, the office of the Registrar, or the Admissions Office. You can also visit the 2019 scholarship page for more information.

E-learning

The University of Embu offers e-learning courses to registered students. Its e-learning platform has a selection of courses from the institution’s five academic schools as well as short online courses. You can study nursing, physical sciences, humanities, business, and agriculture, among other subjects.

To get started, you will first have to enrol as a student. The institution will then give you the credentials you need to access its e-learning resources. You do not need a KUCCPS admission letter to join the university’s e-learning programme. For more information on the programme, visit its dedicated page on the institution’s website.

Student portal

UoEm has a dedicated student portal. The portal is an online system that allows students to access services offered by the institution. Through the University of Embu student portal, scholars can:

  • View timetables and calendars
  • Add course units
  • Check exam results
  • Print exam cards
  • View fee balances and payment history

By default, UoEm creates an account for all new students. The username needed to access the portal is a student’s admission number, and the default password for all accounts is also provided. Once a person logs in, they will have to change their password. If you do not have an account, you can register one from the website.

Accommodation

UoEm offers hostels to all its students. As a new student, you will have to book for accommodation when you join the university. The website offers more information on how to apply for accommodation.
