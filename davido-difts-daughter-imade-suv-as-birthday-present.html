Davido Gifts Daughter, Imade SUV As Birthday Present

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Tuesday, February 13, 2024

Award-winning Nigerian singer, Davido on Thursday presented a Range Rover Evoque to his first child, Imade as her birthday gift.

The Davido Music Worldwide boss disclosed the development in a post on his official Instagram.

Davido who shared two slides on his Instagram page, in the first slide, showed Imade testing her new ride with her friends while in the other slide, the singer shared a picture of the sport utility vehicle, a Range Rover Evoque.

In the caption section, the singer wrote, “Happy early birthday @realimadeadeleke daddy loves u !! Enjoy ur MOTO.”

This is coming days after the singer, via his Instagram, shared a cute photo of his son, Ifeanyi declaring him the sole heir of his empire.
