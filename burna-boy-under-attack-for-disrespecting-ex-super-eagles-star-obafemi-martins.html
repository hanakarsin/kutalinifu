Burna Boy Under Attack For 'Disrespecting' Ex-Super Eagles Star, Obafemi Martins

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

Nigerian singer and songwriter, Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu, better known as Burna Boy, has been dragged and condemned on social media for allegedly ‘disrespecting’ former Super Eagles star, Obafemi Martins.

Naija News understands that trolls on the African Giant started after fellow singer and rapper, Sodiq Abubakar Yusuf, better known as CDQ took to his verified Twitter account to accused Burna Boy of showing no regard for the 36-year-old footballer.

Though CDQ did not state exactly what transpired between Burna Boy and Martins, he however noted in his post that he is disappointed in Burna Boy and his crew, asking them to apologize to the footballer.

CDQ tweeted; “I still can’t phantom this an artist just disrespected Obagoal on thinking he’s now bigger n say e don get mouth pass Obafemi Martins and I imagine how he was able to go back home n sleep comfortably without conscience in this same Lagos igbagbè manshe awa eda ooo”.

“Burna for the first time I’m disappointed in u!!!!! U and ur boys need to go apologize to Obagoal now! No let dem dey deceive u wit ur village title say african gi-ant com dey disrespect Oba Eko lonpe bi. Obafemi Martins is not anybody’s mate. if we dey cry make we dey see

“U don’t disrespect anyone in my circle and I look d other way, no way”.

Coming hot on Burna Boy, a potential footballer identified as Chibueze Austine [@AustineElectt] on the bird app urged the singer to make haste in settling his difference with Martins.
He tweeted: “Burna I think that your red RR car is confusing you and also your infamous dread too.
The gut you have to stand tall before Obagoal is what I can’t explain. Better make haste while there is sunlight else this picture below will define your outcome afterwards.”

In another news earlier, Nigerian singer, David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido is said to have dumped his fiancee and baby mama, Chioma Roland for American model, Mya Yafai.

Naija News reports that Davido and Yafai were spotted in Sint Maarten, a small island in the Caribbean, that is part of the Kingdom of Netherlands for vacation.
