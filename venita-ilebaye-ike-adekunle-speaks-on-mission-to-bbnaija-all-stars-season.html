Venita, Ilebaye, Ike, Adekunle, Speak On Mission To BBNaija 'All-Stars' Season

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Sunday, June 30, 2024

As more housemates are being unveiled for the Big Brother Naija ‘All-Stars’ edition, Venita, llebaye, Ike and Adekunle have revealed their uttermost aims for joining the reality show again.

Naija News reports that the ‘All-Stars’ season, which includes selected former housemates from Pepper Dem, Double Wahala, See Gobe, Shine Ya Eye and other editions, would be battling for the N120 million grand prize.

Apart from being interested in winning the money, IIebaya said she would continue from where she stopped, impact more on her fanbase and put herself out more for people to see who she really is.

Ike Onyema also said he is more interested in money and also spoke about how Lagos State has changed his personality.

Another Housemate, Venita, said she would be more strategic and tactical as well as apply more logic and balance in her game.

Adekunle, on his part, promised to be himself and see how far he would stay in the house.
