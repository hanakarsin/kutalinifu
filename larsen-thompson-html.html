Larsen Thompson - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Thursday, June 27, 2024

Dancer, model, and actress who first gained attention as a dancer in a viral fan video for the hit Beyoncé song "Run the World (Girls)." She has also been in music videos for songs by Borns, Pink, Katy Perry and The Weeknd, and has been a backup dancer for Janet Jackson, Christina Aguilera and French Montana. She appeared as Julia Jayne in the Netflix series The Midnight Club. She also appeared as Kelly in Bloodline and as Maya in the short film Boléro.

Before Fame

She was nine years old when she started working in entertainment professionally. 

Trivia

She has been featured in the New York Times, Harper's Bazaar and Forbes and she has worked with brands like Givenchy, Armani, Carolina Herrera, Coach, Tommy Hilfiger, Dior, Maybelline, Puma, Marc Jacobs and more. She has appeared in independent films such as Pearl and American Cherry. It was announced that she would appear in the 2024 Sony Pictures movie Tarot.  

Family Life

She was born in Thousand Oaks, California and raised alongside her brothers Luke and Cole. Her aunt Julianne McNamara won an Olympic gold medal. She was previously in a relationship with actor Gavin Casalegno.

Associated With

She performed as a backup dancer at the Kids Teen Choice Awards for Christina Aguilera and Pitbull.
