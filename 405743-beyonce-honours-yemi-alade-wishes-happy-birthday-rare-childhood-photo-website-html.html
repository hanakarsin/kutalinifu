Beyonce honours Yemi Alade, wishes her happy birthday with rare childhood photo on website

- Popular American singer and producer, Beyonce, made Yemi Alade's birthday special

- Beyonce in big font wished the Nigerian singer well with a childhood photo of Yemi

- The honour stirred wide reactions on social media

In a separate story, Alade was appointed by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) as their latest goodwill ambassador.

The award-winning artiste’s new role is to help bring to light the impact of poor health and socio-economic systems on the poorest and most vulnerable people which mostly include women in developing countries, UNDP said.

According to the Shekere crooner, women are part of the billions of people in the world who are trying to survive the ravaging COVID-19 pandemic without any social protection.
