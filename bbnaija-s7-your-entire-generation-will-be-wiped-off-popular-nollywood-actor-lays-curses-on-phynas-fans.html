BBNaija S7: 'Your Entire Generation Will Be Wiped Off'

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, March 10, 2024

Popularly Nollywood actor, dina Adinma, has rained heavy curses on fans of Big Brother Naija Season 7 Housemate, Phyna.

Naija News reports that this is coming after Bella and Phyna engaged in a heated argument on Tuesday afternoon and hurled insults at each other.

Reacting to this, dina in a post shared on Twitter, claimed that Phyna was too bossy as the HOH and might possibly beat Bella if not for the BBNaija rules.

He further expressed displeasure at her attitude and likened the reality TV star to a ‘street tout’.

He tweeted: “Phyna be doing too much, if not for big brother rules you go break Bella legs cos you are from the streets lol… na you be first agbero girl…Mtchew”

The tweet earned him criticism from Phyna’s fans and some also stormed his DM to query him over the social media post.

The actor who seemed displeased engaged in a war of words with the fans and rained curses at them.

Bella, Phyna Hurl Hurtful Words At Each Other

Bella, Phyna and Sheggz engaged in a heated argument on Tuesday over food.

Naija News reports that the shouting match broke out after the Head of House, Phyna, convened a general meeting to discuss their weekly wager task.

Instead of focusing on the task ahead, Bella wanted the housemates to discuss the issue of foodstuff which they personally bought and this didn’t sit well with Phyna.

The HOH insist that there should be no other discussion in the meeting aside from the wager task but Bella responded by saying nobody can tell her what to talk about in the house.

She further made a nasty comment saying: “HoH my foot” which infuriated Phyna and called her ‘stupid’.

The two ladies started hurling hurtful words at each other and took nasty swipes at their various backgrounds.

While Bella questioned the place, Phyna was raised,  Phyna also asked about the parents who raised her.

Things escalated between the duo as Sheggz stepped in to defend his girlfriend who didn’t sit well with Groovy, Phyna’s love interest.
