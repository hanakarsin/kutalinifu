Geno Segers - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, July 21, 2024

Actor best known for his role as Mason Makoola on Disney XD's Pair of Kings and as co-host of Fort Boyard: Ultimate Challenge. He has also appeared on MTV's Teen Wolf and Cinemax's Banshee.  

Before Fame

He played rugby professionally in America and New Zealand before beginning his acting career.

Trivia

He had his first film role in the 2013 drama Conversation with Breakfast. 

Family Life

He began dating Maria Canapino in 2011 and he has a son. 

Associated With

He acted with Hutch Dano on two episodes of Zeke and Luther in 2011.
