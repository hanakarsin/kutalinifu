Imo: Supreme Court May Sack Governor Uzodinma

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, April 21, 2024

Primate Elijah Ayodele of the INRI Evangelical Spiritual Church, on Tuesday issues a ‘fearful prophecy’ to the Imo State Governor, Hope Uzodinma.

The cleric in his statement predicted that the Supreme Court may review and reserve its judgment, which pronounced Uzodinma governor of Imo State.

He further stated that he has no knowledge how it will happen but the governor needs to act immediately to prevent the judgement from happening.

Primate Ayodele in a statement released on Tuesday said, “In what seems to be the most fearful prophecy of the year, I have called the attention of the governor of Imo state, Hope Uzodinma to get prepared for a tough time.”

“There will be a review of the Supreme Court judgment that put Hope Uzodimna as Imo State Governor. He must not relent so that he will not see challenges.”

“I don’t know how it will happen but that is what I saw. I saw the judgment being challenged, reviewed and reversed.”

“Hope Uzodinma should be careful because something funny may happen. He should get prepared for a review of the Supreme Court judgment”.

Recall that the Supreme Court sacked the governor-elect, Emeka Ihedioha of the Peoples Democratic Party, and declared Uzodinma winner of the last governorship election in Imo State.
