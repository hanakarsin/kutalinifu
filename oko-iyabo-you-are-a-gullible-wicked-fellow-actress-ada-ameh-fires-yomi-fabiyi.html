Oko Iyabo: 'You Are A Gullible, Wicked Fellow'

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Monday, June 24, 2024

Nollywood Actress, Ada Ameh, has described fellow actor Yomi Fabiyi as a gullible and wicked human being over his newly released controversial movie.

Naija News reports that Fabiyi received backlash from Nigerian celebrities among many others after the release of his new movie ‘Oko Iyabo’, a movie which make reference to comic actor, Baba Ijesha’s rape saga.

Joining hundreds of other Nigerians in condemning Fabiyi for centering the storyline of his new movie around Baba Ijesha’s rape case which is still fresh in the court, Adah Ameh, said the filmmaker lacks family values and that he released the movie just for fame.

She insists that Fabiyi should be flogged for his action. Ameh in her expression described the actor as a self-centred and wicked man who will reap the wickedness he sows.

While calling on the movie censors board to act on the movie, the actress prayed that none of Yomi Fabiyi’s children and those of the actors in the movie experience molestation.

“It is a Shame, do not because of money or cheap fame do the wrong things”, said the Ada Ameh in a video on her Instagram page.

She added: “Yomi it is quite unfortunate that this is how crazy how some people want fame. Bros you call someone your friend; they released him on bail for molestation yet you went to do a film because you want to be popular. We are not going anywhere with this fame; you are supposed to use this fame to help others.

“I used to respect you before but this fame you’re after is the wrong way.

“All the actors on that movie may your children not be victims because you all failed before you started. @realyomifabiyi, you will meet your Waterloo. God almighty will judge you. This is where censors should intervene.

“You’re gullible, wicked and desperate to make a movie out of a case that is still in court; you are worse than the people. May your sons and daughters never be raped.”
