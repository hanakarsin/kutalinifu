Jay Mohr - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Saturday, February 10, 2024

Saturday Night Live star who gained notoriety for his impressions of Sean Penn and Christopher Walken. He also starred in the TV series Gary Unmarried and had supporting roles in such films as Jerry Maguire, Go and Are We There Yet? 

Before Fame

He began his career as a stand-up comedian at age sixteen, performing at colleges, comedy clubs and television forums. 

Trivia

He started hosting the sports talk radio show Jay Mohr Sports in January 2013.

Family Life

He married his second wife Nikki Cox in 2006. He was previously married to Nicole Chamberlain from 1998 to 2004. He has two sons named Jackson and Meredith. He announced his engagement to sports executive Jeanie Buss in December 2022. They married on September 3, 2023.

Associated With

He was a series regular on the TV show Ghost Whisperer alongside Jennifer Love Hewitt.
