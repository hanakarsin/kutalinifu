Makerere University courses, fees, requirements and application process

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, April 18, 2024

Makerere University is a technical school that was founded in the year 1922. Its alumni includes African presidents, prime ministers and other notable personalities. This Ugandan premier institution offers hundreds of certificates, diploma, degree as well as post-graduate courses. Here is everything you need to know about Makerere University courses, requirements and application process.

Makerere University is located on Makerere Hill, one of Uganda’s six hills surrounding Kampala city. Its main campus, which is located a few kilometres from the city square, spans close to 300 acres. Here is everything you need to know about this institution of higher learning.

Makerere University online application

You can apply for any course in this institution without having to go physically to its premises. You will need the following:

  • Internet access
  • Laptop/desktop you can also use a smartphone
  • A browser
  • Email address
  • Scanned copies of passport photo, birth certificate, result slip, previous school I.D., and transcripts where necessary.

How to apply online at Makerere University

Use the following procedure to apply any of the above programs:

  • Visit the Makerere University online application portal.
  • Click on "Register Now" to create a new account.
  • Fill out the registration form with your details exactly as they appear on your academic documents.
  • Make sure the email address and phone number you provide are unique and haven't been used for applications before.
  • After successful registration, you'll be able to log in to the portal.
  • You'll see a list of available programs. Choose the program you're interested in and click "Apply Now."
  • Carefully fill out the application form, paying close attention to mandatory fields.
  • After filling the forms, click on the submit button at the end of the web page.

    Where is Makerere University located?

    Makerere University is located in Kampala, the capital city of Uganda. The main campus is situated on Makerere Hill, about 5 kilometers north of the city center.

    How do you check your admission status at Makerere University?

    You can check your undergraduate admission status via SMS. Text ADM (space) Index No (space) Year of exams. Send the message to 8558.

    • Example text: "ADM U001/123 2024" to 8558. Each text costs Shs 500.

    You can also log in to the Makerere University student portal. Once logged in, you should be able to see your admission status.

    What are the cut-off points for private students at Makerere University?

    Makerere University releases fresh cut-off points every academic year for private students entering various programs. The latest cut-off points available online are for the 2023/2024 academic year. The university hasn't released the cut-off points for the 2024/2025 academic year yet.
