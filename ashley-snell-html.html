Ashley Snell - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Model and entrepreneur most widely recognized for being the wife of professional basketball player Tony Snell, who has played for teams in the National Basketball League including the Chicago Bulls, the Detroit Pistons, the Portland Trail Blazers, and the New Orleans Pelicans. She is the owner and founder of her clothing line, the Ashley Snell Collection, which features coastal cool loungewear available to women of all shapes and sizes, and offers selections from XXS to 4XL. 

Before Fame

She founded her clothing line in 2019, following the resulting trauma from a personal loss that inspired her to create a brand that would empower both herself and her customers as well. Both she and her husband Tony attended Martin Luther King High School in Riverside, CA together and continued to date after graduating. They officially tied the knot in August of 2020.

Trivia

She has been an avid promoter of the Tony Snell Foundation, a nonprofit organization founded by her husband which supports minority children with autism. In October of 2023, she joined the cast of Basketball Wives: Orlando for its 11th season. 

Family Life

She married NBA shooting guard Tony Snell in August of 2020. Together, they have 2 children which were reportedly born through surrogacy. 

Associated With

Some of her castmates on Basketball Wives: Orlando have included Mehgan James, Nikki Nicole, and Mulan Hernandez.
