Davido Pays DJs To Play Only His Songs At US Night Clubs Ghanaian Entertainer

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Thursday, June 20, 2024

American-based Ghanaian singer and promoter, Mr Music Mensah has said that Davido often pay DJs at clubs in the United States to play his songs.

The singer made the claim during an interview with Nana Romeo on Accra Fm as he recounted an incident he witnessed, DklassGH reports.

Mensah said he was at a night club when he saw the singer buying out the club’s patronage.

The “Blessing” singer,m further revealed that Davido paid for everybody’s food and drink, and paid the Dj to play only his songs.

Naija News also recalls that in 2019 controversial Nigerian journalist alleged that DMW boss paid a whopping sum of money to radio houses in the United States.

She tweeted; ”DAVIDO PAID $15M TO MANY US URBAN RADIO STATIONS TO PLAY HIS SONGS ON A 60-DAY MEDIA TOUR. THIS ACT IS CALLED #PAYOLA. YOUR SONGS ARE PUSHED SO HARD TO LAND YOU ON THE CHARTS.”

Davido And Sophia Momodu Block Each Other On Instagram

Davido, and his first baby mama, Sophia Momodu, has blocked each other on the photo and video-sharing app, Instagram.

This is coming a few days after Davido and his ex-fiancee, Chioma sparked reconciliation rumours after they were spotted all loved-up together.

Naija News earlier reported that over the weekend, Chioma broke the net when she stepped out in style for Davido’s concert rocking a new 30BG pendant worth millions.

The pendant had the inscription, ‘002’, which hinted at her reconciliation with Davido.

Another photo that surfaced online on Sunday night captured the mother of one and Davido holding hands together.

In the latest development, Sophia and Davido, who seemed like all have been going on well with them, have unfollowed and blocked each other on Instagram.

This sparked reactions online with some netizens speculating that it might be connected to Davido’s reconciliation with Chioma.
