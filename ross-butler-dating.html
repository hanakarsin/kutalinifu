Who Is Ross Butler Dating? Here's What We Know About the '13 Reasons Why' Star's Love Life

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Monday, May 13, 2024

'13 Reasons Why' actor Ross Butler was spotted wining and dining with another Netflix star in Italy. So, who is the heartthrob dating?

Among the many sexy 20-something and 30-something-year-olds playing teenagers on Netflix's wildly controversial series adaptation of 13 Reasons Why, as well as the CW's cringey Archie comic-adapted Riverdale series, is Ross Butler. The dreamy 31-year-old actor (who should probably move on from portraying high schoolers) landed his breakthrough role as Brett Willis on Disney Channel's series K.C. Undercover, which starred Emmy-winner Zendaya, back in 2015.

The TV star is obviously on his way to potential A-lister status, recently nabbing a voice-acting role in Disney's 2021 animated flick Raya and the Last Dragon. So, considering his promising talent and obviously adorable face, people want to know more about Ross's love life. Well, TMZ recently reported that he was spotted romancing another TV star. The masses (at least of the TV-obsessed variety) want to know, who is he dating?

What does Ross Butler's dating history look like?

“It’s hard because we’ve been in COVID for a year now, so I haven’t gone on any first dates in a while. I’m out of practice. My dating life is a little cold right now,” Ross explained to Glamour back in February 2021. “I’m kind of waiting. I’ve just been working on career stuff and taking the time to learn more hobbies.” The actor, who was the trusted host of small private game nights this past winter, went on about why he wasn't trying to date at the time.

“Since I’m the host of the game night, I don’t want to bring a girl over who isn’t about it or doesn’t want to play. That’s just not going to be fun for anyone, and that’s not a date I want,” he continued. Anyone who isn't into a good ol' game night can take a hike, but that's beside the point.

It seems as though the To All the Boys: P.S. I Still Love You star was playing it a bit coy for his Glamour interview, not giving much info on his dating situation. He did detail that he prefers bowling as a first date over a traditional dinner, however.

Ross, who vaguely relayed that he's interested in women with “a curiosity and appreciation for life,” seems to be a pretty private guy. But with TMZ always at the ready to expose the ins and outs of celebrities' personal lives, there's a new development regarding Ross's dating life.

Who is Ross Butler dating now?

While the relationship has not yet been confirmed, Ross Butler was spotted getting close with Outer Banks star Madelyn Cline, a fellow Netflix hottie (is there a dating app for Netflix hotties or something?). TMZ nabbed a clip of the acting duo dining and dancing in Italy, as they recently attended star-studded Milan Fashion Week events. You know, just hot famous people things.

Fans of the Netflix drama were shocked when rumors swirled that Madelyn, who will appear in the highly anticipated Knives Out 2, and her Outer Banks castmate Chase Stokes called it quits, but we have to admit, she looks quite good alongside Ross. For now, it looks like Ross and Madelyn are having fun, and we wish them all the best. As for Chase Stokes, call us?
