Jessica Baio - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, February 29, 2024

Young pop singer and songwriter who has released singles by the names of "My Angel," "Go With the Flow," "I Wish You Would Stay," "Do You Remember," "Mine" and "Loving You."

Before Fame

She began talking voice lessons when she was 10 years old.

Trivia

She posts covers of top artist's songs on YouTube, including her very popular cover of Miley Cyrus' "Wrecking Ball" that she sang when she was 11 years old.

Family Life

Her full name is Jessica Courtney Baio. She was raised in a Mormon family alongside two brothers and one sister. She runs a YouTube channel called Sam & Jess with Sam Jose, whom she got engaged to in June 2020. They got married on November 1, 2020.

Associated With

She has covered artists such as Jessie J, John Legend, Nicki Minaj and 5 Seconds of Summer, among others.
