See Why Peter Obi And Son Are Trending On Social Media

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, May 27, 2024

Former Governor of Anambra State, Peter Obi, has become the subject of intense online discussion following the alleged sexual orientation of his son, Oseloka Obi, sparking both support and criticism.

While the veracity of the claims remains unconfirmed, the issue has raised important questions about privacy, acceptance, and the role of public figures in social discourse, Naija News reports.

Recall that Peter Obi was the candidate of the Labour Party in the 2023 presidential election.

Obi and the likes of Atiku Abubakar of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), however, were defeated by All Progressives Congress (APC) candidate, President Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

However, months after the election defeat and the end of court processes, Obi is now enmeshed in social media discussions over his son’s alleged gay status.

Viral photos seen online show Oseloka featuring on the front page of a magazine in what seems like promoting gay. Another shows him with another man lurking into each other with inscriptions that look like a movie advert.

However, while some netizens suspected of being Obi’s supporters are emphasizing the importance of respecting individual privacy and avoiding discrimination based on sexual orientation, some have continued to mock the politician and his supporters.

Naija News understands that Obi himself has yet to publicly address the matter as of the time of filing this report.
