Faux locs styles

1. Purple yarn locs

Purple yarn locs are without a doubt some of the most common Faux locs Kenya hairstyles adopted by ladies. If you are daring enough, this style can suit you. The purple color is eye catching and meant to draw people’s attention. Styling faux locs of this nature is quite easy and you can choose to have your locs short or long depending on your preferences.

2. Faux locs bob

Long faux locs are the norm in the society, aren’t they ladies? If you love to keep your hair short then the bob faux locs style is what you need to consider. Other than saving you the burden of long, tedious hair on your head, the bob faux locs will also protect your hairline. If you are looking for short faux locs hairstyle, I would highly recommend this style to you.

3. Crochet faux locs

Faux locs crochet hair styles have become the in thing in Kenya right now. If you are a lady and you have not tried out this style then you don’t know what you are missing out on. While other fancy hairstyle helps you stand out from the crowd, Faux locs crochet offers more. With this style on your head you could forget all your hair problems at least for a year. With no irritations whatsoever you can naturally take care of your hair with this Faux locs styling, what more could you ask for?

Another Faux locs styles 2018 ladies need to try are the short red faux locs. However, I should caution that this style is prefect for outdoor and not the office. The light skins have a higher advantage as this faux locs style perfectly complements their skin tone.

Normal is boring right? You can always play with your faux locs to achieve that desired look you have always wanted. One of the best ways about this is by waving your locs.
