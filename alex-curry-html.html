Alex Curry - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

Reporter known for being the host of Angels Weekly and Kings Weekly. She's also covered The World University Games for Fox College Sports as well as the Pro Beach Volleyball Series for NBC Sports.

Before Fame

She studied Journalism at San Diego State University and worked for Fuel TV while in school. She worked for Last Call With Carson Daly after graduating before joining Fox Sports West in 2012.

Trivia

She played soccer in college and won a NISRA title with the team in 2006. Her family is very health-conscious and she volunteers for Growing Great, an organization that encourages healthy eating.

Family Life

She has three younger sisters. Her father, Tim Curry, founded a natural energy drink company and her mother, Peggy, founded Growing Great.

Associated With

She's been part of the LA Kings broadcast crew with Jim Fox and Bob Miller since 2013.
