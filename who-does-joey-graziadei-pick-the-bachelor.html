Who Does Joey Graziadei Pick on The Bachelor? SPOILERS!

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, June 30, 2024

Joey Graziadei Finds His "Lifelong Doubles Partner" on 'The Bachelor' (SPOILERS)

Reality Steve shared an update about who wins Joey's final rose in Season 28 of 'The Bachelor.'

By Allison DeGrushe

Feb. 27 2024, Updated 10:07 a.m. ET

Spoiler alert: This article contains MAJOR spoilers for Season 28 of The Bachelor.

As we excitedly tune in to follow the charming heartthrob Joey Graziadei's quest for true love unfold every week on Season 28 of The Bachelor, some of us just can't resist the urge to skip the suspense. We're on a mission to uncover every juicy spoiler and spill the piping-hot tea on how this love story reaches its grand finale.

If you're as eager as we are to uncover the mystery of Joey's final pick, don't click away because we've got the answer straight from the resident spoiler king, Reality Steve. Don't miss out — check it out below!

Who does Joey Graziadei pick on 'The Bachelor'?

Alright, buckle up for the juiciest scoop! Now, we can't guarantee these spoilers are foolproof, but Reality Steve's track record is pretty spot-on. So, chances are, we're diving into the real deal.

And now, the moment you've all been eagerly awaiting: On Nov. 29, 2023, Reality Steve reported that Joey picks Daisy Kent! However (and that's a big however), he shared on Feb. 27, 2024, that he was wrong. Yes, we know, insert shocked Pikachu meme here.

Instead, according to Reality Steve, Joey actually ends up with Kelsey Anderson. She walks away with the final rose and a sparkly engagement ring from Joey.

Fast forward to the last few days of the season, and Joey — accompanied by his final three women — whisks off to the enchanting Tulum, Mexico, for overnight dates. After saying goodbye to Rachel Nance, the leading man introduces his final two ladies — Daisy Kent and Kelsey Anderson — to his family.

The details of these final dates are shrouded in secrecy, but one thing's crystal clear: Joey's finale is going to be full of tears one way or another.

And so, despite the dramatic hints from the trailers, it seems Joey's season is sticking to the traditional Bachelor finale script, complete with an emotional proposal. Ugh — we can't wait to see this classic Bachelor ending!

Who is Kelsey A. from Season 28 of 'The Bachelor'?

Although Kelsey A. isn't the first impression rose recipient, and she doesn't snag the first kiss of the season, it doesn't take long for her to grow closer to Joey. So much so that she opens up about the loss of her mom to cancer and her relationship with her dad now. She lives in New Orleans and she's a project manager for a living, but something tells us she would have no problem giving that up to start a new life with Joey.

Which, if the spoilers are correct, is what she does by the end of the season. As with all of the other seasons of The Bachelor, our lead in Season 28 hasn't confirmed the new rumor about who he ends up with. But as of now, it's hard to deny the connection that Joey and Kelsey A. share.
