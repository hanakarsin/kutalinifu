Trevor Ombija Leaves Fans in Awe after Presenting News in Swahili

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Thursday, February 29, 2024
  • Citizen TV news anchor Trevor Ombija joined his colleague Mashirima Kapombe on Sema na Citizen to present news in Swahili
  • The collaboration was coupled with comical moments as Ombija, who is used to presenting news in English broke some ground rules of Swahili news presenting
  • Many fans on social media were amused by how Ombija struggled to get the job done and they camped at the comment section divided in opinions

News anchoring may appear as simple as ABC but if Trevo Ombija's performance during Sema na Citizen is anything to go by, then it is not for all.

It all started when Mashirima Kapombe who presents news in Swahili invited an English news presenter for a Swahili show.

Ombija could be seen struggling to read news in Swahili. He tried his best to be as flawless as he is in presenting news in English but to no avail.

Going to Instagram, Mashirima asked netizens to give their opinions on whether the Monday Report host should join the Swahili news gang or not.

Netizens gave different opinions after watching the clip as some applauded Ombija's performance while others told him to stick to his tuff.

Victoria Rubadiri, Jeff Koinange dance in studio

In another related story published on TUKO.co.ke, Citizen TV news anchors Jeff Koinange and Victoria Rubadiri recently showed off some moves as they celebrated Eliud Kipchoge and the Kenyan team at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics for the good performance.

As they completed Sunday, August 8, prime time news bulletin on Citizen TV, the two introduced their dance as a tribute to the Olympics team before busting their moves.

Dressed in different shades of blue, the duo could be seen moving from side to side with huge smiles on their faces as they tried to synchronise their moves.
