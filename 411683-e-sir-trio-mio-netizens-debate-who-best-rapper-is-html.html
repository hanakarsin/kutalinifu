E-Sir or Trio Mio? Netizens Debate Who the Best Rapper Is

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, August 16, 2024

- E-sir has been gone for almost 20 years but he is still considered one of Kenya's top rappers of all time

- Trio Mio's emergence as a budding talent has drawn comparisons to the South C legend

- Social media users have been discussing who the best rapper is

Since making a splash with Cheza Kama Wewe, budding singer Trio Mio has drawn comparisons with the legendary E-sir.

The high school student was accepted by established artistes, including Nyashinski, Khaligraph Jones and Bien-Aimé Baraza.

Khaligraph even hosted Trio, and they did a freestyle, with the youngster showing he can keep up with the best.

From his flow, delivery, to choice of words, many opine Trio is more lyrically advanced than his peers.

Trio’s E-sir comparisons have dominated the debate on Twitter for the better part of the day, with netizens expressing varied opinions.
