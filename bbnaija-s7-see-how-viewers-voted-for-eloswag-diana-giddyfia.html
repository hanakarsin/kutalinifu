See How Viewers Voted For Eloswag, Diana, Giddyfia

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Sunday, July 14, 2024

The organizers of the Big Brother Naija Season 7 have released details of how viewers voted for their favourite housemates last week.

Naija News reports that this is coming following the eviction of Diana and Giddyfia from the reality show on Sunday.

Recall that all the housemates were put up for possible eviction after Amaka was suddenly evicted on Monday.

During the Sunday live show today, Giddyfia and Diana were evicted alongside the fake housemate, Deji.

Giddyfia and Diana had the least votes and were evicted from the reality TV show.

Here is how viewers voted:

1. Eloswag- 2.13%

2. Daina- 1.45%

2. Giddyfia – 0. 99 %

Ned Nwoko’s Estranged Wife, Laila Tenders Apology

Meanwhile, the fifth wife of billionaire businessman, Ned Nwoko, Laila Charani has tendered an unreserved apology to him and his family members months after the dramatic separation.

It would be recalled that in October, Laila publicly disclosed that she was divorced from the politician.

“Hello Guys, I just want to say that no one has the right to talk about my marriage. Please stop talking about people’s lives I have divorced him already and nothing between me and him. Anybody following me because of him can unfollow, please. Thanks. I’ll block any negative comment”, she revealed.

Ned Nwoko on the other hand, while clarifying the factors that led to the divorce in a statement said, “The neglect of the kids, violence on them, plastic surgery without the knowledge of her husband, wayward lifestyle abroad, contact with a man, police case and involvement of a lawyer in London were the major concerns”

However, in a post on her Instastory, Laila apologized to her husband, blaming her actions on outsiders who influenced her.

She further noted that her actions were not intentional, adding she realized that she made mistakes.

She said that she wants peace and progress in the family hence the apology.
