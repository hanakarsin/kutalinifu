What happened to Grant Thompson? 'The King of Random' cause of death

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, June 7, 2024

"The King of Random" YouTube star Grant Thompson passed on after being involved in a paragliding accident. Grant Thompson will be remembered as the creator of YouTube’s popular channel, "The King of Random". As a YouTube star, Thompson showed great love and appreciation for his followers which gave him a huge fan base. It is, therefore, a great loss not only to his family but also to the global community he single-handedly created. What happened to Grant Thompson is tragic, and we all put him in our prayers.

The sad news popped online as his death was announced on his social media accounts. What did Grant Thompson die of?

"The King of Random" creator died in a paragliding accident. He was paragliding in Utah but failed to return home. Grant Thompson’s family were concerned, and they filed a missing person report with the police. His body was recovered the following day in St. George, Utah. Officials were able to locate his body thanks to a GPS locator that was in his possession.

According to the New York Post, an unknown caller reported to the police regarding his disappearance. The authorities were able to recover the body with paragliding equipment and a recording device. The police are reviewing the video footage to help establish what might have been the cause of the tragic accident. However, it was left to the doctors to determine Grant Thompson's cause of death.

The King of Random

Before Grant Thompson's death, he was popularly known as the creator of "The King of Random", a YouTube channel that posts random experimental videos and clips about everyday life hacks and projects. Many of his videos have over 1 million viewership making him a popular figure on YouTube. New York Post claims the star had 11.6 million subscribers in his channel.

What you probably didn’t know about Grant Thompson

There are many Grant Thompson wiki questions regarding his life before social media. Grant Thompson was born in 1980 and has died at the age of 38. His most popular video, How to Make LEGO Gummy Candy!, was published in May 2015. The video showed how one can make candy at home, and it has more than 34 million views to date.

Before fame, Grant Thompson tried different careers before settling as a Social Media Star. According to a 2017 interview with the website Media Kix, Mr. Thompson revealed that he previously worked as an airline pilot for 11 years. He then left the airline industry and proceeded into a real estate business that involved buying and selling houses. The business earned him enough money to retire and engage in other things partially. That is how he started making videos on YouTube to show people the things he was doing.
